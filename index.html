Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. Check out our FAQ section to find what you're looking for.

What kind of opportunities can I find on the Under 25 app?

expand_more

The Under 25 app is your gateway to a wide range of opportunities, including exclusive internships, part-time jobs, freelance projects, and volunteering roles from top companies and startups. We also host various competitions and challenges to help you showcase your skills.

Is the Under 25 app free to use?

expand_more

Yes, the Under 25 app is completely free for all students to download and use. Our mission is to provide equal access to opportunities and resources to help every student succeed.

How does the community feature work?

expand_more

Our community feature allows you to connect with like-minded students from across the country. You can join interest-based groups (Spaces), participate in discussions, collaborate on projects, and find mentors to guide you on your journey. It's a great way to network and learn from your peers.

Can I use the Under 25 app to build my portfolio?

expand_more

Absolutely! By participating in freelance gigs, internships, and competitions, you can build a strong portfolio of your work. The app provides a platform to showcase your projects and skills to potential employers and collaborators.
