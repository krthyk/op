About Me
Namaste! I am a Mumbai based designer who is deeply obsessed with the intersection of culture and technology in modern India. Over the last six years I have focused on building digital experiences that help Indian startups scale their vision through clean and user centric design. When I am not iterating on Figma you will usually find me documenting the hidden street food gems of Mumbai or exploring the emerging indie music scene across the country. My ultimate goal is to simplify complex problems one pixel at a time while staying true to the vibrant aesthetic of our local culture.
